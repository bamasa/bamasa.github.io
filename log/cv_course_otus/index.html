Course: Computer Vision (OTUS)

September 25, 2020 One-minute read
computer vision • courses • certificates

I took a course on Сomputer Vision from OTUS (in Russian) from April 28 to September 25, 2020. The course was practical and covered Neural Networks and Deep Learning in the field of Computer Vision - from the basics to the latest frameworks and state-of-the-art scientific articles. Besides that, there were cool lessons about TensorRT and Server Inference. In addition, the interesting thing about this course was to learn from the experience of colleagues who use Computer Vision in different fields. In the end, I did an interesting project on the topic “Animated face expression tracking”.
