Space Navigator

One-minute read
LAMBDA, HSE

TL; DR

We made Space Navigator - a system for automatically determining the probability of collision of space objects and calculating the most effective evasive maneuver using AI.

Stage of the project:

  • Research Article “Space Navigator: a Tool for the Optimization of Collision Avoidance Maneuvers” (2020)
  • MVP (2019)
  • First place in the competition of projects of the Academy of Roscosmos (presentation slides, 2018)
  • VR presentation (2018)

Demonstrations and reports:

  • “SMILES”, summer school (Moscow, Skoltech, 2020)
  • “Space debris: fundamental and practical aspects of the threat”, Russian conference with international participation (Moscow, Institute of Astronomy RAS and Space Research Institute RAS, 2019)
  • “IAA SciTech Forum”, international conference (Moscow, RUDN, 2018)
